About PC Remote

A simple tool for a simple problem.

Why This Exists

Ever been watching something on your PC from across the room and wished you could pause it from your phone? Or needed to scroll through a presentation without walking back to your desk? That's why PC Remote exists.

What It Does

PC Remote turns your phone into a wireless controller for your PC. It runs over your local WiFi — no internet needed, no apps to install on your phone, no accounts to create.

  • Media controls — play, pause, skip, volume, mute, fullscreen
  • Virtual trackpad — move your mouse by swiping
  • Click, right-click, scroll, double-click
  • On-screen keyboard — type from your phone
  • Mouse 4 & 5 buttons for browser navigation

How It's Built

The desktop app is written in Python using Flask as a lightweight local server and pyautogui for input simulation. The phone interface is a responsive web page — no frameworks, just HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.

The entire project is open source. You can read every line of code, build it yourself, or modify it however you like.
