Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

PC Remote is a local network tool that lets you control your PC from your phone. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (spoiler: none).

Data Collection

PC Remote does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking of any kind.

How It Works

All communication happens directly between your phone and your PC over your local WiFi network. No data passes through any external server. When you close the app, the connection ends and nothing is saved.

Third-Party Services

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser type) as part of their hosting service. This is standard for any website and is governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

The .exe Application

The desktop application runs entirely on your local machine. It does not phone home, check for updates, or send any data anywhere. The source code is open and available for inspection on GitHub.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top. Given the nature of this tool, significant changes are unlikely.
